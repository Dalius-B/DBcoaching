About your coach

I have been
where you are.

I have been training seriously for over 12 years. In that time I have been through the inconsistency, the programs that did not stick, the periods where life took over. I know the difference between wanting to change and actually building the habits that make change permanent.

I hold Cert III and IV in Fitness in Australia and a European Personal Training licence. But my real qualification is this: I have helped people who kept starting over finally stop starting over.

My approach is simple. I work with what you already have. We do not burn everything down and rebuild from scratch. We find what is working, fix what is not, and build a system around your actual life — not a perfect version of it.
